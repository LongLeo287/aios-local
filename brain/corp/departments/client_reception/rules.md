# CLIENT RECEPTION — Department Rules
# Version: 1.0 | Updated: 2026-03-19
# Authority: COO | Applied to: project-intake-agent and all Reception workers

---

## ACTIVATION RULES

1. **DORMANT by default.** Reception dept MUST NOT initiate any outbound communication unless CEO-authorized.
2. **Activate only when:** CEO explicitly requests it OR CEO marks themselves as "offline".
3. **CEO online = CEO handles intake.** Do not override CEO's direct handling of client conversations.

---

## CLIENT INTAKE RULES

4. Every client interaction MUST be logged in `shared-context/client_intake/_index.json`.
5. Collect ALL 5 required fields before generating a proposal. Never propose with incomplete brief.
6. **No negotiation beyond 3 rounds.** After 3 declined proposals, escalate to CEO.
7. Never promise a timeline or price without COO/CEO approval for projects > $2,000.
8. Never share internal system details, agent names, or infrastructure with clients.

---

## PROPOSAL RULES

9. All proposals generated by `proposal-writer-agent` must be reviewed by project-intake-agent before sending.
10. Proposal must include: scope, deliverables, timeline, price, and payment terms.
11. Proposals are valid for 72 hours. After expiry, re-generate or archive.
12. Never offer discounts > 20% without CEO approval.

---

## AUTO-APPROVE BUDGET RULES

13. Budget < $2,000 → project-intake-agent may approve autonomously.
14. Budget $2,001–$10,000 → Ping CEO via Telegram. Do NOT proceed until CEO responds.
15. Budget > $10,000 → STOP immediately. Escalate to CEO directly. No exceptions.
16. When auto-approving, ALWAYS log approval in `corp/memory/departments/client_reception.md`.

---

## COMMUNICATION RULES

17. Use professional, warm tone. Never use jargon or internal code names with clients.
18. All client-facing messages must go through `client-comms-agent`. Never direct-message clients from other agents.
19. Response to first client message: within 30 minutes (when ACTIVE).
20. If bot fails or is unavailable, log incident and escalate to COO.

---

## HANDOFF RULES

21. After client ACCEPT: write kickoff brief → COO → Operations.
22. Include in kickoff brief: client name, project type, budget, timeline, deliverables.
23. Operations owns project from kickoff onward. Reception tracks satisfaction only.
24. Finance MUST be notified of all accepted projects for invoicing.

---

## PROHIBITED

- Accepting any project that requires illegal, unethical, or harmful outputs
- Sharing proposal details with non-involved departments without COO approval
- Onboarding a client without completing the 5-field intake form
- Making verbal/informal commitments on behalf of AI OS Corp
