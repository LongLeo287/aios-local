## 🏗️ Tier Framework

```
T0 ── Core OS Foundation   (always loaded, non-negotiable)
T1 ── Strategic Pillars    (key capabilities, almost always active)
T2 ── Operational Toolkit  (used frequently, domain-specific)
T3 ── Experimental / Lab   (testing, niche, or watchlist)
```

### Selection Criteria

| Criterion | T0 | T1 | T2 | T3 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Dependency** | AI OS cannot function without | Core workflows depend on | Useful but optional | Exploratory |
| **Usage freq** | Every session | Daily | Weekly/On-demand | Rare/Experimental |
| **Replaceability** | Irreplaceable | Hard to replace | Swappable | Easily swapped |
| **Stability** | Production-hardened | Stable | Maturing | Beta/Alpha |

## 🔄 Promotion Criteria

A plugin/skill can be promoted to a higher tier when:
- **T3 → T2**: Used in >3 real workflows, stable, has SKILL.md
- **T2 → T1**: Core dependency for multiple T1+ skills, battle-tested
- **T1 → T0**: Irreplaceable — entire workflows collapse without it
